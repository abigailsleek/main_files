At gift a pad, we are a reflection of each other, we have found a way to feel, breath and to heal each other, with kindness, with love, with soothing words, in chastisement, and with our resources.

Our membership will open to you a window of opportunity to find fulfilment in serving humanity with your little contributions in service, time, financial and material resources.

We are completely non-profit and exist only to ensure that every girl child have access to proper menstrual hygiene. Also, to push for legislations for a nation-wide government responsiveness towards the achievement of this objective.

We do honestly desire that you be a part of this noble fight, so together we can spread kindness, give gifts of hope, self-esteem, and confidence. Join us to raise a nation where kindness is a culture and care is the attitude, because that is the spirit we inspire in the lifetime of this noble cause.

You are welcome!

We look forward to sharing more of our goals with you!
